How To Make A PB&J

                               

What Is A PB&J?

Peanut butter and jelly sandwiches, also known as PB&J sandwiches are a classic American staple. These simple yet delicious sandwiches provide a creamy, nutty flavor, and a sweet, fruity taste. The combination of these two flavors creates a perfect balance that is both satisfying and comforting. I have been personally making PB&J sandwiches since I was a kid and enjoy them every time. A good PB&J is quick to make, tasty, and is without a doubt one of the best sandwiches of all time.






How To Make A PB&J

Ingredients:

- 2 Slices Bread(preferably white bread)

- 1 tbsp Peanut Butter

- 1 tbsp Jam (preferably strawberry jam)




Recipe:

1. Spread Peanut Butter evenly on one side of one slice of bread.

2. Spread Jam evenly on one side of other piece of bread.

3. Close pieces of bread, peanut butter side touching Jam side.

4. Enjoy!


(For extra crunch, toast in a lightly buttered pan untill both sides are golden brown)
