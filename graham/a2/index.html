Avocado Toast Recipe


Introduction

Avocado toast is a great start to anyone's day, as it's one of the best easy breakfast meals there is to offer. It's my favorite breakfast dish because it is very tasteful and easy to make.


Ingredients

  • 1 Avocado
  • 1 Slice of bread
  • ½ Lemon
  • A Pinch of salt
  • 1 Cutting Board
  • 1 Spoon and Knife

Instructions

  1. Put your slice of bread into the toaster
  2. Cut your Avocado in ½ around the pit
  3. Take out the pit and scoop the insides onto a cutting board
  4. Dice the parts on the cutting board until you have many small pieces
  5. Take your slice of bread out of the toaster and put the diced avocado bits on the toast
  6. Press and spread the diced bits along the toast with a spoon
  7. Squeeze the juices of the lemon half onto the toast
  8. Spread the salt over the toast
  9. Eat the Avocado Toast
