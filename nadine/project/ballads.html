Ballads 1

Released on October 26, 2018, Ballads 1 became Joji's debut studio album. This collection of ballads embodies introspection, incorporating themes revolving the reality of love, life, and everything in between. George expresses vulnerability, unrequited love, dishonesty, and change in his failed relationship. In an interview with Annie Mac, he said that he was trying to make each song different in their "own special way" and that are powerful, setting a new direction but keeping the "Joji roots" he established on In Tongues. Miller stated in another interview with Triple J how he wanted to take a more pop-oriented approach to his style on the album, which began with the single "Yeah Right".
