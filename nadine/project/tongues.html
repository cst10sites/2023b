In Tongues

Released on November 3rd 2017, In Tongues was George Miller's debut EP as Joji. His album depicts feelings of sadness and heartbreak, exemplified by strangling and drowing in the cover. All six tracks focus on regret, melancholia, and bitterness. Their soundscapes create a textural musical experience: dusty piano samples, lo-fi hip-hop drums, percussive foley sounds, and ethereal vocals meld together to create haunting instrumentals. The atmosphere is suffocating, drawing listeners immediately into memories of failed relationships and dark periods of their lives.
