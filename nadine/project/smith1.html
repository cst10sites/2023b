In Tongues Ballads 1 Nectar Smithereens
Guess the Song! About Joji

Description

Released on November 4th, 2022, the album is divided into two sides, with the first side containing introspective ballads, while the second side, contains lo-fi sounds. Again, Joji sings about heartbreak, trying to either move past it, fighting to get it back, or projecting those feelings onto someone new, looking for a glimpse of what he experienced with his previous partner. Much like Ballads 1, Smithereens makes perfect use of the airy elements of bedroom pop and synths to create tracks very characteristic of Joji. He maintains the constant use of distinctive features such as analogue synths, pensive piano ballads, and dreamy vocals in all the tracks, but we can also see him use experimental trap and electronic beats to keep the album interesting.
