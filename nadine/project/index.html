Guess the Song! About Joji

    Description

  • George Kusonoki Miller, known as Joji, Filthy Frank, and Pink Guy is a Japanese singer-songwriter, former comedian, and Youtuber. Miller integrates the R&B, lo-fi and trip hop genres that eventually rebranded him as a serious artist after years of establishing an Internet personality.

  • Born in Osaka, Joji left Japan and travelled to the United States at the age of 18. He built a massive online following in the early 2010s with his comedy skits, with his Harlem Shake becoming a viral phenomenon. Eventually, George grew out of the characters he played, claiming that naturally as he got older, he got tired of that humour. While Joji had a passion for music, he would incorporate it in his Pink Guy era but were mostly comical. As a result, he began writing music that was compelling with its moody tones and authentic lyricism. In 2017, George came up with a new stage name, Joji.
