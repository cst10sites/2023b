Guess the Song! About Joji

Nectar

Released on September 25, 2020, Nectar encompasses all the feelings of heartache and what people are willing to risk for love. In this album, Joji sings of bitter emotions in sweet-sounding melodies. Nectar is described as “a sense of urgency, but calm” by Joji. The album contains a unique production style, deep lyricism about heartache and how dark the music industry really is. Overall, Joji’s sophomore album represents his growth as a musician because it is a cohesive piece that shows his artistic abilities as a songwriter and producer. Nectar is a mature take on relationships, and Joji proved that there could never be too many songs about love.
