Buko Salad Recipe

Today we'll be making Buko Salad, one of my favorite Filipino desserts that my mom would make over the holidays. It's known for its creamy, sweet texture, as well as its refreshing flavors.



Ingredients

  1. 4 cups of shredded coconut (buko)
  2. 6 ounces of sugar palm fruit (kaong), drained
  3. 12 ounces of coconut gel (nata de coco), drained
  4. 2 cups of fruit cocktail, drained
  5. 7 ½ ounces of pineapple chunks, drained
  6. 1 ¼ ounce of sweetened condensed milk
  7. 7 ounces of all purpose cream, chilled

Instructions

  1. Combine the coconut shreds, sugar palm fruit, coconut gel, pineapple chunks, and fruit cocktail in a mixing bowl. Gently stir to distribute the ingredients.
  2. Add sweetened condensed milk and table cream. Mix until all the ingredients are properly distributed.
  3. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or place in the freezer for 1 hour.
  4. Transfer to a serving bowl.
  5. Enjoy ☺
