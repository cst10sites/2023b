Recipe for Blueberry Muffins

Introduction

This is a quick and easy recipe for Blueberry Muffins that the whole family can enjoy. I enjoy this recipe as they are delicious and I can make them easily whenever I want. This recipe is good for anytime of day as they can be eaten for breakfast, a snack or even dessert.

This recipe makes either 20 to 22 mini muffins, 8 large big top muffins or 10 standard muffins (which is what I usually use).

INGREDIENTS

  • 1½ cups of all purpose flour
  • ¾ cup of granulated sugar
  • ¼ tsp of fine salt
  • 2 tsp of baking powder
  • ⅓ cup of oil
  • 1 large egg
  • ⅓ to ½ a cup of milk
  • 1½ tsps of vanilla extract
  • 1 cup of fresh or frozen blueberries

COOKING INSTRUCTIONS

  1. First mix all the dry ingredients together
  2. In a seperate bowl mix all the wet ingredients together
  3. Put all of the dry and wet ingredients together in one bowl and mix
  4. Gently add and fold in the blueberries
  5. Bake muffins for 15-20 minutes at 400°f

ENJOY!
