Strawberry Banana Smoothie Recipe

Introduction

Strawberry banana smoothie is a recipe that will cure your thirst in the summer. With its fresh and delicious taste and simple ingredients, you can make them anytime you want! That's why I have picked this for my recipe because it's my favourite drink throughout the seasons. It is so refreshing, healthy, and tasty. This website will teach you to become a barista and make this delicious treat for your family and friends.

                                                                                           

Ingredients

  • 5 strawberries
  • 1 whole banana
  • ¾ to 1½ cup of milk depends on the thickness that you prefer
  • 1 tbs of sweetend condense milk (Optional)

Instructions

  1. Wash your strawberries and remove the leaves on each one.
  2. Cut your banana into quarters for an easier blend.
  3. Once done, pour all your ingredients into a blender.
  4. Turn your blender on and put it at medium speed.
  5. After everything is blended, pour it into a cup.
  6. Enjoy your freshly-made strawberry banana smoothie!
