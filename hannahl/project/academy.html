Rafa Nadal Academy

The Place to Prepare Players for the Future of Tennis

"It is important to recognize small succeses." - Rafael Nadal

Introduction

The Rafa Nadal Academy was created by Rafael Nadal, Toni Nadal, and other coaches. The idea is to build a facility for Rafa to train at while promoting his name around the world, but at the same time giving a chance to all the kids in Manacor and around the world a fair experience to improve their tennis skills. This international academy combines tennis and education so that students can pursue tennis while keeping up with their studies. With junior and adult programs, anyone who is interested in tennis can apply.

Once in a program, the academy offers top-notch service and experience. There are multiple tennis courts inside and outside throughout the 24,000 square feet of land. Also an indoor and outdoor swimming pool. Students can stay at the Rafa Nadal residence and are provided 5 stars service from the coaches, physicists, therapists, and nutritionists.

With the amazing facilities offered here, many stars were born. All stars that have trained here became known around the world and achieved great awards in their tennis tours. Below are famous names that have trained at the Rafa Nadal Academy:

  • Rafael Nadal
  • Casper Ruud
  • Andy Murray
  • Bianca Andreescu
  • Emil Ruusvuori
  • Felix Auger-Aliassime
