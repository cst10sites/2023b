The Ideal Mango Pudding

Mango puddings are sweet, and bursting with a fresh mango flavor. Ever since the first time I tasted it, I fell in love with mangoes. Now I want to share this tasty dessert with many people, so you can enjoy it too! Today, I am going to share with you the recipe for a Chinese mango pudding, that will make your taste buds fly.

Mango Pudding Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Unflavoured Gelatin
  • White sugar
  • Salt
  • Coconut Milk
  • Milk
  • Fresh Mangoes

Instructions:

  1. Cut mango with a knife and dice it into pieces.
  2. Place the mango flesh in the blender. Add in ¼ cup of water.
  3. Blend the mango until it becomes a smooth puree.
  4. Pour the pureed mango through a strainer.
  5. Add the milk to the puree.
  6. Put ⅓ cup of cold water in a small pan.
  7. Dissolve the gelatin in the cold water.
  8. Let it sit for five minutes.
  9. Now add the sugar into the gelatin. Heat it slowly until the sugar dissolves.
  10. Slowly pour the gelatin mixture into the mango puree mixture.
  11. Pour it through the strainer again
  12. Put a cup of diced mangoes in the container.
  13. Fill up the molds with the mixture.
  14. Use the tip of a napkin to break bubbles.
  15. Cover with cling film and chill until the pudding is set.
  16. Add more mango cubes on top of pudding for decoration.
