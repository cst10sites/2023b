My Recipe

Introduction

This recipe is for my favorite omurice, becuase of delicious fried rice with egg sheet and savory sauce. The dish is a perfect combination of textures and flavors, with fluffy fried rice, soft egg sheet, and a flavorful sauce that brings everything together. Here's a picture of the dish:

Ingredients

  • For the sauce:
    1. 1 tbsp (10g) flour
    2. 2 tbsp (15g) cooking oil
    3. 2 tbsp (30g) ketchup
    4. 2 tbsp (20g) soy sauce
    5. 1.5 tbsp (15g) brown sugar
    6. 1 tbsp (10g) vinegar
    7. 1 cup (180g) water
    8. ⅓ tbsp (3g) beef dashida
  • For the fried rice:
    1. ⅙ (30g) onion
    2. ¼ cup (20g) carrot
    3. 1 bowl of rice
    4. 1 tbsp soy sauce
    5. 2 tbsp Mirim
    6. 2 tbsp cooking oil
    7. Pepper
  • For the egg sheet:
    1. 3 eggs
    2. Cooking oil

Instructions

  • Make the sauce:
    1. Heat the cooking oil in a saucepan over medium heat.
    2. Add the flour and stir constantly for 1 minute to make a roux.
    3. Add the ketchup, soy sauce, brown sugar, vinegar, water, and beef dashida, and whisk until the mixture is smooth.
    4. Cook the sauce over medium heat for 5-10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until it thickens.
  • Make the fried rice:
    1. Finely dice the onion and carrot.
    2. Add cooking oil to a pan and stir-fry the onion and carrot.
    3. Once the vegetables are cooked, push them aside, and scorch the soy sauce and Mirim.
    4. Stir-fry the soy sauce and vegetables together, add the rice, and sprinkle pepper.
    5. Shape the rice in a dome shape on the plate.
  • Make the egg sheet:
    1. Beat the eggs in a bowl.
    2. Add cooking oil to a pan and pour in the eggs when the pan is heated.
    3. Gently stir if as making scrambled eggs and cook the eggs halfway.
    4. Place the egg sheet over the rice.
