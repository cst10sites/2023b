My Recipe

Introduction

In this recipe you will be learning how to make macaroni and cheese. Now this is a very basic meal but if made right it can be enjoyed by yourself or with others. The reason I chose this food is because it's something I grew up with and enjoy to this day.


Ingredients

  • 1 (8 ounce) box elbow macaroni
  • ¼ cup of butter
  • ¼ cup of all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon of salt
  • ground black pepper to your liking
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 2 cups of shredded Cheddar cheese

Instructions

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil at 100°C. Cook the elbow macaroni until al dente, about 8 minutes.
  2. While the macaroni is cooking, go ahead and start on the roux. A roux is a thickening agent made of one part fat (butter) and one part flour. This roux makes up the base of this creamy and delicious macaroni and cheese.
    Start by melting your butter in a pan over medium heat. Then add the flour, salt, and pepper and stir until its smooth (no lumps). Proceed to slowly pour in milk and stir until the mixture is smooth and bubbling. Be careful to not let the milk burn. Continuously stir throughout this whole process.
  3. Add the cheese into your Roux that you have made and slowly stir until its smooth and melted. Graded cheese will get incorporated into the mixture easier than pre-shredded cheese.
  4. Finally, once the macaroni is finished cooking, drain it and then stir it into the amazing cheese sauce until it's coated.
