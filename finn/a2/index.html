My Recipie

Introduction

The recipie that I'll be sharing is my hot chocolate recipie. It has a very rich taste, and isn't too sweet, unlike most hot chocolate mixes used by restaurants and cafes.

Ingredients

  • 3 spoons of cocoa powder
  • 2 spoons of sweetener (like sugar or maple syrup)
  • As many cups of milk as you'd like to drink (two or three is a good amount in my opinion)
  • A pinch of salt
  • A shake of cinnamon (optional)

Instructions

  • First, add water to the bottom half of a double boiler
  • Add milk & the other ingredients to the top half
  • Mix with a whisk, until the liquid is smooth
  • Set stove dial to "high"
  • Serve when liquid starts to emit steam (I don't know exactly how long it takes, but it's usually < 10 minutes
