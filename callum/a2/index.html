Quesadilla Recipe

Introduction

The food I will be making is a quesadilla. I am making a quesadilla because they are very easy to make and they are delicous, I usually make them 2-3 times a week.

Ingredients

  • ½ Cup of cheddar cheese
  • ½ Cup of mozzarella cheese
  • ½ Cup of jalapeño jack cheese
  • Soft taco shell

Instructions

  1. Put a ½ cup of cheddar, mozzarella, and jalapeño jack cheese into a soft taco shell, then fold it in half.
  2. Place the quesadilla into the panini grill and wait 5 minutes.
  3. Take the quesadilla out of the panini grill with a spatula and place the quesadilla on a cutting board.
  4. Cut the quesadilla into 4 pieces.
  5. Eat your delectable creation.☻
