Strawberry Banana Smoothie

Introduction

First of all, strawberry banana smoothies have been a delicacy to me for all my life. Ever since the first-time visiting McDonalds and purchasing this sweet, healthy, and wholesome smoothie, I have been hooked. Who wouldn't want to make a quick and cheap smoothie with no sugar added that ALSO contains healthy nutrients? Well follow me and make a simple recipe that consists of only strawberry bananas, Greek yogurt, and ice! This easy recipe will blow your mind.☺

Ingredients

Recipe is for 1 serving

  • ½ cup of crushed ice
  • 1 banana sliced, frozen
  • 1 & ½ of strawberries frozen
  • ⅓ cup of Greek yogurt

Instructions

  1. Add ice, Greek yogurt, bananas, and strawberries to a high-speed blender.
  2. Blend
  3. Serve and enjoy!
