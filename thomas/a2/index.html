Thomas's Special Eggs

Step 1

Does not mater what kind of eggs that you use. Start by grabbing 2 eggs per person then beat the eggs till mixed.

Step 2

After eggs are mixed add salt and peper to your mixed eggs.

Step 3

Heat a pan and add 2 tbsp butter to the pan at medium heat.

Step 4

Slowy pour your eggs into pan and then sprinkle some chopped up oregano and some shredded cheese on to your eggs.
