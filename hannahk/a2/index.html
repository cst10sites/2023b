My Recipe

Introduction

Annie's mac and cheese is one of my favorite foods because it is quick, easy to make, and delicious. It is my after school snack or food to make for dinner when I'm feeling lazy and don't know what else to make.

Ingredients

  • Annie's box mac and cheese
  • ¼ cup milk
  • 2 tbsp butter

Instructions

  1. Boil 6 cups of water to 100°C in a medium saucepan.
  2. Stir in pasta and bring to 100°C and cook for 8-10 minutes, stirring ocdivly.
  3. Drain pasta in colander. While pasta is draining...
  4. Add ¼ cup milk and 2 tbsp butter to the warm saucepan.
  5. Sprinkle cheese packet over milk; stir to combine.
  6. Add cooked pasta back to saucepan, stir well, and you're done!
