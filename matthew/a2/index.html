 𝐼𝓃𝑔𝓇𝑒𝒹𝒾𝑒𝓃𝓉𝓈 (𝟪 𝓈𝑒𝓇𝓋𝒾𝓃𝑔𝓈)

   Veggies

  • 2 eggplants
  • 6 roma tomatoes
  • 2 yellow squashes
  • 2 zucchinis

   Sauce

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, diced
  • salt, to taste
  • pepper, to taste
  • 28 oz can of crushed tomatoes (795 g)
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh basil, 8-10 leaves

   Herb Seasoning

  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh basil, 8-10 leaves
  • 1 tsp garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 tsp fresh thyme
  • salt, to taste
  • pepper, to taste
  • 4 tbsp olive oil

 

 𝒯𝒽𝑒 𝒫𝓇𝑜𝒸𝑒𝓈𝓈

  1. Preheat oven to 190°C (375°F).
  2. Slice eggplant, tomatoes, squash, and zucchini into 1⁄16-inch rounds (1mm).
  3. Make sauce:
    1. Heat olive oil in 30cm (12 inch) oven-safe pan over medium-high heat.
    2. Sauté onion, garlic, and bell peppers until soft (approx. 10 minutes).
    3. Season with salt and pepper to liking.
    4. Add crushed tomatoes.
    5. Stir more, then smooth surface of sauce with spatula.
  4. Arrange sliced veggies in alternating patterns (ex. eggplant, tomato, squash, zucchini) on top of sauce from outer edge to middle of pan. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Make herb seasoning:
    1. Mix basil, garlic, parsley, thyme, salt, pepper, and olive oil in small bowl.
    2. Spoon herb seasoning over vegetables.
  6. Cover pan with foil and bake for 40 minutes.
  7. Uncover, then bake for another 20 minutes until vegetables are softened.
  8. Serve while hot. Still excellent the next day. (Cover with foil and reheat in 180°C (350°F) oven for 15 minutes, or microwave to desired temperature.)
  9. Nom nom nom!
