Frank Lloyd Wright

Fallingwater

















Designed in 1935, Fallingwater is perhaps Wrights most famous building. Fallingwater is a 9300 square foot building (including the outdoor terraces) which boasts a limited color palate to seem more organic to the nature it resides in. Built in southwest pensylvania over a stream, Fallingwater is built of stone, concrete, glass and wood. It is currently open to visitors.
