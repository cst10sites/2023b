Grilled Cheese




Introduction

I have been eating grilled cheese since I was a young child. Grilled cheese is a staple in not only my household but many more across North America and the world. Whenever I eat grilled cheese, it reminds me of coming back from soccer practice and having a warm meal to come home to. Now, let's get into the recipe.


Ingredients

  • Cheddar Cheese ½ Cup
  • Monterey Jack ⅓ Cup
  • Butter ⅓ Stick
  • White Bread 2 Slices
  • Parsley Handful


Procedure

  1. To start, spread the butter on both sides of each slice of bread.
  2. Place a handful of cheddar and Monterey jack on one slice, and place the other slice of bread on top.
  3. Place a pan on the burner and let it heat up on medium for 1 minute.
  4. Put your sandwich on the preheated pan and apply moderate pressure. Release the pressure and allow it to cook for 3 minutes on one side.
  5. Flip the sandwich with a spatula and repeat step 4 for the other side.
  6. Cut the sandwich diagonally and plate it with some sprigs of parsley for presentation.
