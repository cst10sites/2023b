Geography:

Spain is a country located at the south of Europe. It shares borders with Portugal (west side), with France (north) and Andorra which is a small country also at the north. Spain and Portugal form the Iberian Peninsula. Madrid is the capital of Spain and is in the middle of the peninsula. Spain also has some overseas territories:

  • Canary Islands, which are at the west of Morroco.
  • Balearic Islands, which are at the east of Spain.

Language:

In Spain the official language is Castellano. But there are some co-official languages like Catalan, spoken in cities like Barcelona and in the Balearic Islands, Valenciano spoken in Valencia, Euskera spoken in different cities like Bilbao and Gallego spoken in Galicia.
