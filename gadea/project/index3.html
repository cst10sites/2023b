SPAIN

Landscapes

Oceanic climate Alpine climate Continental climate Mediterranean climate
The north of Spain has long beaches, with big waves, but also a lot of meadows where farmers usually have their animals. The north also has a lot of mountains, and green landscapes. The interior is drier, if you're on a road trip you will probably see farmlands, and the landscapes will mostly be warm colors. Finally, we have the coast. The beaches are not as long as in the north, and the waves are not as big either, but the water is perfect for snorkeling and swimming.
