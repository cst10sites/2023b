Dumplings

I chose dumplings because I love them and they are very easy to make, if you have the right ingredients. I used to have them for dinner at least once a week because me and brother love them, and now it reminds me of him. It's been so long since I've had them, and I think it would be nice to remember the recipe by putting it in this assignment.

 

 

Ingredients you will need:

  • 1 pack of dumplings dough (16 units)
  • 200 grams of tuna
  • 2 boiled eggs
  • 3 and ½ tablespoons of tomato sauce
  • Egg wash

 

Steps of the recipe:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C.
  2. Boil 2 eggs for 10 minutes.
  3. Mix the tomato sauce and the tuna.
  4. When the eggs are boiled, cut them into little squares and put it with the tuna and tomato sauce.
  5. Put 1 tablespoon of filling in the middle of each unit of dumpling dough.
  6. Fold the dough and seal it with a fork.
  7. Brush the dumpling with egg wash.
  8. Put the dumplings in the oven for 12 minutes.
