SEVENTEEN is an ultra-large group with 13 members and 3 units, meaning that they form a team. Preparing for their debut with a differentiated promotion that introduced a pre-debut system and opened their pre-debut training process to the public, SEVENTEEN made their first steps into the music industry with their first mini album "17 CARAT" in May 2015 and quickly became super rookies. Three different units (hip-hop team, vocal team, and performance team) coexist in the group, and each team participates in the overall production of the album, living up to the label of self-produced idols. Through music and performance that only SEVENTEEN can show, the group has gone from being labeled as a super rookie, self-produced idol, and refreshing idol shortly after their debut, to being a popular idol in Korea and abroad, further solidifying their position in the music industry.
